STEPHEN

2015

I got the inspiration for this piece when looking through DIYs on Pinterest of picture collages and during the Warriors playoff run I decided to try my hand at it. I ripped up many different types of scrapbook paper and then glued and collaged them all into a rough outline of Steph Curry to create a solid image.
